
## 1. Resume Roaster / Optimizer
**What:** Upload a resume + paste a job description. AI scores the match, rewrites weak bullets, flags ATS killers, and gives a "hire probability" score.
**Monetization:** $5–15/month subscription or pay-per-resume credits ($2–5 each).
**Why it works:** Job seekers are desperate and will pay. High search volume. Low churn — one-time purchase model also viable.
**AI angle:** Claude/GPT rewrites resume sections, scores keyword density, generates tailored cover letters.
